> Pressing [W, A, S, D] plays the corresponding sing animation.\nHolding shift while doing so will attempt to play alt animations.
> Pressing [Z, X] will move a frame forward/backward of the currently playing animation.
> Pressing C will replay the current animation.
> Pressing [Q, E] can zoom in/out the camera.
> You can Press CTRL+Z and CTRL+X to undo and redo changes.

Mouse Controls:
> Click and hold the mouse wheel to drag the camera and scroll to zoom.
> Holding the right mouse button will let you drag the current animations offset.
> Hovering over the camera pointer and holding left click will allow you to drag it.
